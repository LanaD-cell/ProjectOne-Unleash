Parks & Restaurants

Something for everyone

At UNleash we endevour to expand you and your beloved dogs life by finding hidden gems in the cities you live in. We, as dog owners, know how limited our urban roaming space can become. There are only a handful of walking trails and even fewer social opportunities ,ie. restaurants, hotels etc., life can become pretty longwinded pretty fast for ourselves and our dogs.

Have a look at some great places we found in and around the city.


Social Butterfly Park

Welcome to Central City Park, the ultimate playground for both dogs and their owners in the heart of the city. With its expansive lawns, winding trails, and scenic spots, it’s a dog-walking paradise where your furry friend can run, play, and explore to their heart’s content. From the lush fields of the Great Lawn to the wooded paths of the Ramble, every corner of the park offers new adventures for dogs of all sizes. For those looking to socialize, the designated dog runs provide a safe space for your pup to meet new friends. Whether you're leisurely strolling around the Reservoir or enjoying a game of fetch, Central Park is the perfect place to bond with your dog while experiencing the beauty of nature in the city.

Dippers Delight

The ultimate park designed exclusively for swimming dogs! This unique, off-leash haven is the perfect place for your furry friend to splash, swim, and play in a safe, dog-friendly environment. With crystal-clear waters, spacious beach areas, and plenty of play zones, Paws & Waves offers a fun, refreshing retreat for dogs of all shapes and sizes. Whether your dog loves to paddle in the shallow waters or dive into deeper pools, they’ll enjoy hours of aquatic adventure, exercise, and socializing with other pups. Come make a splash today—your dog’s new favorite swimming spot awaits!

Adrenalin Junkie

An unforgettable adventure where the beauty of the mountains meets the joy of exploring with your furry companion. This scenic walking trail takes you through lush forests, across babbling streams, and offers breathtaking panoramic views of towering peaks. Whether you're embarking on a leisurely hike or tackling a more challenging ascent, Summit Trails is perfect for you and your dog to bond with nature. After a day of adventure, set up camp at one of our dog-friendly sites, where your pup can relax under the stars. Ready for a paws-itively perfect escape? The mountains are calling!

Herders Haven

Where your dog can discover their natural instincts and learn the art of herding! Set against the backdrop of rolling fields and open pastures, our farm offers a unique experience for dogs to train and develop their herding skills. Under the guidance of experienced trainers, your pup will get hands-on practice with our friendly flock of sheep, learning how to control and guide them in a safe and fun environment. Whether you're looking to bond with your dog through a new activity or help them tap into their herding potential, Herding Paws Farm is the perfect place for both you and your dog to experience the thrill of the herd!

Doghouse Cafe

Where great food and furry friends come together! Our dog-friendly restaurant is the perfect spot to enjoy a delicious meal while your pup enjoys their own special treats. With a cozy, outdoor patio and a welcoming atmosphere, both you and your dog can relax, sip, and savor the moment. We offer a menu full of tasty options for humans, plus a selection of dog-approved dishes, from gourmet treats to refreshing pup-cakes. Bring your dog along for a fun dining experience—they’ll be treated like family here at The Doghouse Café!

Bark & Bite

The perfect spot where your dog is as much a guest as you are! At our dog-friendly restaurant, we believe that dining out should be a shared experience. Enjoy a delicious meal from our chef-driven menu while your pup lounges by your side, sampling their own special treats. With a welcoming atmosphere, outdoor seating, and a variety of doggy delights, Bark & Bite is the ultimate destination for both you and your four-legged companion. So bring your pup, sit back, and enjoy a paws-itively delightful dining experience!
