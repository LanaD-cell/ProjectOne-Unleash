Parks

Something for everyone

At UNLEASH we endevour to expand you and your beloved dogs life by finding hidden gems in the cities you live in. We, as dog owners7companions, know how limited our urban roaming space can become. When there are only a handful of walking trails and even fewer social oppertunities ,ie. restaurants, hotels etc., life can become pretty longwinded pretty fast. for ourselves and our dogs.

Have a look at some great places we found in and around the city.

Herders Haven

Herders Haven offers a unique service. They offer training for young herding breed dogs and herding outings for trained herding dogs. A super fun option is "Holiday worker", your dog can stay for a short holiday and work the herds under the guidens of a professional shephard.

Visit Us

Adventure Seekers Hiking Park

This park offers too many adrenalin indusing options for the adventurous dog. Well marked trails up and down the mountainous range. Camoing and cleaning facilities near all entrances.
